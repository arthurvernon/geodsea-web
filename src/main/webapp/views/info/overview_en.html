Overview

Geo d'Sea provides the boating community with communication and monitoring tools that save both time and money.

If you are a:

  • Skipper: You can keep sea rescue, your friends and family informed about your journey. This service will also allow you to receive notices relevant to your present location.
  • Sea Rescue Organisation (SRO): This service will allow you to maintain a watch and manage your area of operations. SROs are informed when a vessel is overdue and can use the system to obtain information on the vessel including its last reported location and the number or poeple on board.
  • A Friend or Family Member: By registering for this service, the skipper may then authorise you to monitor his location from the time he logs on he logs off.
  • Club: You can use Geo d'Sea to provide a real-time or after then event geographical view of the event.
